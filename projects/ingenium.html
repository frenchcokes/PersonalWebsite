Ingenium

Overview

A roguelike game about being a student. Gameplay is dragging blocks to decide what actions to do for the day based on upcoming assignments and tests. There are also random text events where you must make choices based on your stats.

Used tools: Unity, C#

Features

  • Semi-randomized gameplay
  • Unlockables
  • Text events and choices
  • Clickable buttons
